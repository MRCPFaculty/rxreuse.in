About RxReuse

We're on a mission to make healthcare accessible by bridging the gap between surplus medicines and those in need.

Our Mission

Where Sharing Becomes Healing

RxReuse is India's premier medicine donation NGO dedicated to preventing medicine wastage while ensuring affordable healthcare for underprivileged patients.

Every year, thousands of rupees worth of unused, unexpired medicines go to waste in Indian households, while millions struggle to afford basic treatments. We create a transparent ecosystem connecting donors with patients in need.

Through rigorous volunteer verification and a transparent tracking system, we ensure every donated medicine reaches genuine patients safely and efficiently.
