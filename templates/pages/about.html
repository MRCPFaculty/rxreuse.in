About RxReuse

RxReuse is a non-profit initiative dedicated to bridging the gap between surplus medicines and patients in need.

Our mission is to ensure that no medicine goes to waste while someone, somewhere, is struggling to afford their prescription.

How We Work

  • Donors ship their unused, unexpired medicines to our warehouse.
  • Volunteers verify the quality and safety of every donation.
  • Patients apply for aid and receive medicines for free, paying only for shipping.
